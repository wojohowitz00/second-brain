# Research Topics

Configure your research interests here. The Research Engine skill uses this to generate daily digests.

## How to Add Topics

For each topic you want to track:

```markdown
## Topic: [Name]
Keywords: keyword1, keyword2, keyword3
Categories: cs.AI, cs.LG (arXiv categories, optional)
Priority: high/medium/low
```

## Example Topics

## Topic: AI Agents
Keywords: AI agents, autonomous agents, agentic AI, multi-agent systems
Categories: cs.AI, cs.MA
Priority: high

## Topic: Personal Knowledge Management
Keywords: personal knowledge management, second brain, note-taking, PKM
Priority: medium

## Topic: Productivity Research
Keywords: productivity, time management, cognitive load, attention management
Priority: low
